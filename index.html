Account Deletion Request

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your game history, achievements, and rankings will be permanently removed.

How to Delete Your Account

Required Information

  • WordDuel Username: Your current username in the game
  • Registered Email: The email address must match your WordDuel account
  • Confirmation Statement: "I confirm that I want to delete my WordDuel account"

Email Template Example

Subject: WordDuel Account Deletion Request

Username: [Your WordDuel Username]
Email: [Your Registered Email]

I confirm that I want to delete my WordDuel account.

Reason (Optional):
[Your reason for leaving]

What Happens Next

Step 1: Send your deletion request

Step 2: Receive confirmation email within 24 hours

Step 3: Account deletion processed within 48 hours

Step 4: Final confirmation email sent
